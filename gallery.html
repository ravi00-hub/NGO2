About Us

Insaniyat Foundation is a grassroots social organization dedicated to promoting legal awareness, public health, social responsibility, and human dignity. Through health camps, cleanliness drives, environmental protection, blanket distribution, and women’s safety initiatives, we work to create stronger communities, encourage justice, and build a more compassionate society.
